Keyboard Teleoperation
===========

Try out our simple teleoperation setup - with just your keyboard!

Movement Controls
-----------------
+---------------+------------------+
| Key           | Action           |
+===============+==================+
| :kbd:`h`      | ``+`` roll       |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`k`      | ``-`` roll       |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`y`      | ``+`` pitch      |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`g`      | ``-`` pitch      |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`b`      | ``+`` yaw        |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`n`      | ``-`` yaw        |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`o`      | open gripper     |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`p`      | close gripper    |
+---------------+------------------+

Camera Controls
-----------------
+---------------+------------------+
| Key           | Action           |
+===============+==================+
| :kbd:`i`      | show information |

+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`s`      | save image       |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`z`      | reset camera     |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`a`      | camera rotation  |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`h`      | shadow           |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`f`      | face normal      |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`v`      | vertex normal    |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`w`      | world frame      |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`l`      | link frame       |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`d`      | wireframe        |
+---------------+------------------+
| :kbd:`c`      | camera & frustrum|
+---------------+------------------+
